Spicy Jollof

Description

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Seasoning
  • Groundut oil
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Plantain(optional)

Steps

  1. Ensure all needed utensils are available and clean
  2. Pour about a litre of water into your pot and place it on fire.
  3. Empty your tomato paste and groundnut oil into the pot of water
  4. Slice your onions into it also and cover the pot.
  5. Pour 5 cups of rice into a bowl and rinse to remove particles of dirt.
  6. Pour the already rinsed rice into the pot and leave for 10 moinutes
  7. After exactly 10 minutes, squeeze two cubes of seasoning into the simmering rice and add a pinch of salt.
  8. Stir properly. Let your spoon reach the depths of the pot as you stir.
  9. Cover the pot while it cooks.
  10. In 20 minutes, food is ready. Enjoy!
